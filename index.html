What is a Discord Bot?

Discord bots are automated programs that perform tasks within a server, including moderation, leveling, ticket management, custom commands, and more.

How it works: Bots interact with the Discord API to respond to messages, reactions, joins, and other events, executing commands as per your server's rules.

Benefits: Save time, engage your community, automate repetitive tasks, and provide a professional experience to your server members.
